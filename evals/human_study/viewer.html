GeoGenBench rating

Rate ~200 (prompt, diagram) pairs against the geometry verifier.

What you'll do

  • Q1 (overall): answered blind — auto-verdict hidden until you submit.
  • Q2 (per-property): for each predicate the verifier checked, agree / disagree / unsure.
  • Notes are optional — record edge cases or disagreement reasoning.

Throughput tips

  • Keyboard: 123 for Q1, adu for Q2, ←→ to navigate, ? for help.
  • Auto-saves on every click — the indicator at top-right shows status.
  • Stop and resume any time. Progress is restored from the server.
Naming convention: use rater_a or rater_b so analysis picks up your CSV without flags. Other names work too — pass --raters <your-name>,<partner-name> to compute_kappa.
